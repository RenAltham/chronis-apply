Universe

THE CHRONIS CODEX

When the Avatars — beings who each embody a fundamental aspect of existence — merged their Cores into a single object, The Chronis was born. It became the absolute center of all realities, multiverses, timelines, and dimensions. It has dominion over everything. Everything except Chaos.

⏳

THE AVATARS

Each Avatar represents a pillar of existence. Kronii is Time — the warden who governs the flow of all moments. Mumei is Civilization — the memory of every society that ever rose or fell. Sana is Space — the infinite canvas upon which reality is drawn. Their Cores are fragments of their domain's fundamental power.

🔎

AMELIA WATSON

A time-displaced detective and the protagonist of the Chronis series. Amelia carries the weight of a mission that spans every possible reality: to find a way to revive her closest friend, Sherry Locke — who is dead across every single timeline. Sarcastic, resourceful, and haunted by what she's lost.

💀

THE GREAT COINCIDENCE

An event so catastrophic it shattered the concept of infinity. Every possible version of Amelia was connected to every possible version of Sherry — an impossibility that overloaded the universe itself, collapsing all timelines into a single, irreversible outcome.

> RECOVERED FRAGMENT — TIMELINE ΔΔ-0
> "She died everywhere. At the same time. In every when.
> Not because the universe wanted her gone —
> but because it couldn't hold the weight of how
> much she mattered to every version of me."
> — A.W., Case File ∞
🌀

CHAOS

The one force The Chronis cannot control. Chaos is not evil — it's indifferent. It exists outside the framework of order, reality, and reason. It does not oppose. It simply is. And it finds the whole situation rather amusing.
