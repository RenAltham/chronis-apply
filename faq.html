Everything you need to know about the Chronis universe and Timeborne Interactive.

WHAT IS CHRONIS?

The Chronis Game Series is an original franchise of games built in Unreal Engine 5 by Timeborne Interactive. The series follows Amelia Watson, a time-displaced detective navigating a multiverse held together by an artifact called The Chronis — the merged Cores of beings known as the Avatars.

The series explores themes of loss, connection, and the cost of infinity. Multiple titles are in development spanning different genres and perspectives within the same universe.

THE STUDIO

Timeborne Interactive is an indie studio founded by Ren (RenHatesCode). We build games, create content, and cultivate a community of people who are way too invested in fictional timelines. We're small, scrappy, and building something real.

FAQ

When does Chronis: Omega release?▾

We don't have a release date yet. We're focused on getting it right, not getting it out fast. Follow our Roadmap for the latest development status.

What platforms will the games be on?▾

PC (Windows) is the primary target. Console ports are a possibility down the line but not confirmed.

Can I join the team?▾

We're always open to talented people who are passionate about what we're building. Check the Apply page or reach out on Discord.

What's the Dossier system?▾

The Dossier is an interactive lore database. Entries are locked by default — you unlock them by finding secret codes hidden in our YouTube videos, Discord, streams, and across this website. It's basically an ARG built into the site.

Is the lore based on Hololive?▾

The Chronis series is an original work. While character names and concepts are inspired by Hololive Council lore, the story, universe, and games are entirely original creations by Timeborne Interactive.

How can I appear in a YouTube video?▾

Fill out an application on the Apply page. We review applications manually and contact selected participants via Discord.

I found a bug on the site / have feedback▾

Drop it in the feedback channel on our Discord server. We read everything.
