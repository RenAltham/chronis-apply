DESCRIPTIONS

SHORT (1 LINE)
The Chronis Game Series is an original multiverse franchise following detective Amelia Watson as she searches for a way to revive her friend across infinite timelines.
MEDIUM (PARAGRAPH)
The Chronis Game Series, developed by Timeborne Interactive in Unreal Engine 5, is an original franchise set in a multiverse governed by an all-powerful artifact called The Chronis. The series follows Amelia Watson, a time-displaced detective on a mission to revive her closest friend Sherry Locke — who is dead across every timeline due to a catastrophic event known as The Great Coincidence. Spanning multiple titles and genres, the series explores themes of loss, connection, and the cost of infinity.
